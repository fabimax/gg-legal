Privacy Policy for GoodGuilds

Last Updated: [5 March 2025]

1. Introduction

Welcome to GoodGuilds ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect information in relation to our website, services, and applications (collectively, the "Services").

By accessing or using our Services, you consent to the collection, transfer, storage, disclosure, and use of your information as described in this Privacy Policy. This includes any information you might provide through our Services.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

Account Information:

  • Email address (collected during registration or through social login providers including Twitter/X)
  • Username and password (for email registrations)
  • Profile information (name, bio, avatar, etc.)

Guild Information:

  • Guild names, descriptions, and other content you create
  • Membership information and roles within guilds

Communication Information:

  • Information you provide when contacting us
  • Feedback, suggestions, or other communications

2.2 Information Collected Through Social Login

When you register or log in through Twitter/X or other social media platforms, we collect:

  • Your Twitter/X username and unique identifier
  • Your email address associated with your Twitter/X account
  • Your profile picture (if available)
  • Any other information you authorize Twitter/X to share

Why We Request Email Addresses: We collect email addresses to:

  • Enable account recovery
  • Send important service notifications
  • Facilitate guild invitations
  • Allow communication between guild members
  • Provide badge notifications and updates

2.3 Information Collected Automatically

Usage Information:

  • Log data (IP address, browser type, pages visited, time and date of visits)
  • Device information (hardware model, operating system)
  • Cookies and similar technologies

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Provide, maintain, and improve our Services
  • Process guild creation, membership, and badge assignments
  • Send notifications and updates about guild activities
  • Respond to your comments, questions, and requests
  • Send technical notices, security alerts, and support messages
  • Enable communication between users for guild-related purposes
  • Monitor and analyze trends, usage, and activities
  • Protect the security and integrity of our Services
  • Comply with legal obligations

Specifically, your email address is used for:

  • Account verification and security
  • Password reset and account recovery
  • Essential service communications
  • Guild invitations and notifications
  • Badge award notifications
  • Updates about guilds you belong to

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

4.1 Information Visible to Other Users

Certain information is visible to other users of our Services:

  • Your username and profile information
  • Guilds you belong to and your roles within them
  • Badges you've earned (that you choose to display)

4.2 Information Shared with Third Parties

Service Providers:
We may share information with third-party vendors and service providers who work on our behalf to help provide our Services (hosting, database, analytics, etc.).

API Ecosystem:

  • Badge issuers may receive limited profile information when assigning badges
  • Third-party applications may access guild membership information through our API as authorized by you
  • Your email address is never shared with badge issuers or third-party applications unless you explicitly consent

Legal Requirements:
We may disclose information if required by law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect the information we collect against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. These measures include:

  • Encryption of sensitive data in transit and at rest
  • Access controls limiting data access to authorized personnel
  • Regular security assessments and testing
  • Employee training on data protection practices

However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you with our Services. We will also retain and use your information as necessary to:

  • Comply with our legal obligations
  • Resolve disputes
  • Enforce our agreements

If you delete your account, we will delete or anonymize your information within 30 days, except for information that we are required to retain for legal purposes.

7. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: You can request access to the personal information we hold about you
  • Correction: You can request correction of inaccurate information
  • Deletion: You can request deletion of your information (subject to certain exceptions)
  • Objection: You can object to certain processing of your information
  • Restriction: You can request restriction of processing in certain circumstances
  • Data Portability: You can request a copy of your information in a structured, commonly used, machine-readable format

To exercise these rights, please contact us at admin@goodguilds.com.

8. Email Communications

We send several types of emails:

  • Service Communications: Essential notifications about your account, guild membership, badge awards, and security updates
  • Marketing Communications: Updates about new features or services (you can opt out of these)

You can manage your email preferences through your account settings or by following the unsubscribe instructions included in each email.

9. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than the one in which you reside. These countries may have different data protection laws.

When we transfer information outside of your country, we implement appropriate safeguards in accordance with applicable law.

10. Children's Privacy

Our Services are not directed to children under the age of 13 (or the applicable age of digital consent in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you become aware that a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

12. Twitter/X Data Usage

When you connect your Twitter/X account to GoodGuilds, we request access to your email address solely for the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy. We follow Twitter/X's Developer Agreement and Policy regarding data usage. Specifically:

  • We only request the minimum data necessary to provide our Services
  • We clearly disclose how we use the information collected through Twitter/X
  • We do not use Twitter/X data for purposes unrelated to the functionality of our platform
  • We protect Twitter/X user data with the same standards we apply to all user data
  • We do not sell Twitter/X user data or use it for advertising purposes without explicit consent
